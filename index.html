About me

Charlie Hudson

Software Developer from London

A budding final year Computer Science undergraduate at the University of Bath. I am a highly motivated, creative and hardworking individual with a wide variety of skills in computing. I aim to pursue a career that allows me to get my teeth stuck into the latest and innovative technologies.
